# Sobriety - Enhanced Sobriety Tracker

A clean, supportive, and private sobriety tracking application designed to help you stay motivated on your journey. Built with a focus on simplicity, privacy, and user experience.

![Sobriety Icon](favicon.svg)

## 🌟 Key Features

- **Live Counter**: Track your sobriety down to the minute with a dynamic live timer.
- **Financial Savings**: Automatically calculates how much money you've saved based on your previous weekly spend.
- **Daily Check-ins**: Log your success or slips, track your mood, and record the intensity of your urges.
- **Supportive Journaling**: Keep private notes about your progress, struggles, and reflections.
- **Health Timeline**: Visualize how your body recovers over time with a detailed health benefits roadmap.
- **Coping Tools**:
  - **4-7-8 Breathing Exercise**: An interactive guide to help ground you during difficult moments.
  - **Emergency Support**: A dedicated section for quick access to support lines and personal reasons for staying sober.
  - **Healthy Distractions**: A customizable list of activities to help you navigate cravings.
- **Data Privacy**: All your data is stored locally in your browser using `localStorage`. No accounts, no servers, no tracking.
- **Dark Mode**: A beautiful, eye-friendly dark theme that automatically syncs with your preferences.
- **Milestones & Rewards**: Reach key milestones and celebrate your progress with interactive confetti and badges.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **HTML5**: Semantic structure.
- **Vanilla CSS**: Custom design system with glassmorphism and modern aesthetics.
- **Vanilla JavaScript**: Core logic, routing, and data persistence.
- **Chart.js**: Visualizing mood and urge history.
- **Local Storage**: Secure, client-side data persistence.

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Clone or Download**: Save the project files to your local machine.
2. **Open index.html**: Simply open `index.html` in any modern web browser.
3. **Onboarding**: Set your quit date and weekly spend to begin your journey.

## 💾 Data Management

You can easily **Export** your data to a JSON file for backup or **Import** it if you switch devices. Look for these options in the **Resources** tab.
